An ultra-premium, modern minimalist landing page for EnerGen Solutions, a leading B2B service provider in the Oil & Gas industry. The audience is C-level executives and Asset Managers. It must exude operational efficiency, reliability, and absolute trust. 

**STITCH DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Palette: 
  - Primary Accent: EnerGen Blue (#5BB8DF)
  - Secondary Accent: EnerGen Orange (#F05A28)
  - Backgrounds: Stark White (#FFFFFF) for main content, Slate-900 (#0f172a) for massive high-contrast Hero and Footer sections.
- Typographic style: "Inter" font. Massive `font-black` headers with tight tracking. Use minimal border colors (e.g., slate-200) to separate components with significant `py-24` and `gap-16` spacing.
- Styles: `rounded-2xl` for big containers, `rounded-sm` for buttons. Very soft, elegant glassmorphism (`backdrop-blur-lg`) for sticky navs and overlaid cards. High contrast. Zero visual clutter. No chaotic borders or playful dropshadows.

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header:** Sticky transparent glassmorphic navigation (Home, Services, About, Careers, Contact).
2. **Hero:** Huge dark Slate-900 background. Headline "Harness Excellence In Your Asset Management & Supply Chain Operations". Subhead "Global Expertise. Local Efficiency. Customized Evolution." Two sturdy buttons: Primary (Energen Orange, "Explore Services"), Secondary (White outline, "Contact Us").
3. **Introduction / Global Footprint:** Crisp white section. "Welcome to EnerGen Solutions Limited". A map visualization or stat block showcasing 15+ years of experience and global presence. 
4. **Services Teaser:** Two large, elegant glassmorphic cards for "Asset Management Solutions" and "Supply Chain Management Solutions". Clean typography and subtle shadow-sm.
5. **Footer:** Dark Slate-900. Simple navigation, location, and social links.
